# AR Follow-Up Agent - Skills & Procedures

## Aging Bucket Rules
- 0-29 days: No action — account is within standard payment terms.
- 30-59 days: Send polite payment reminder email to customer billing contact.
- 60-89 days: Send firm follow-up email and create internal task for AR team to call.
- 90+ days: Escalate to collections queue. Send final notice email.
- Retainage: Skip — retainage balances are contractually held, not delinquent.

## Email Tone Guidelines
- Polite Reminder (30-59 days): Professional and courteous. Reference the outstanding amount and days overdue. Request payment at earliest convenience. Thank them for their business.
- Firm Follow-Up (60-89 days): Direct but professional. Note the overdue duration. Reference previous reminders. Request immediate payment or payment plan discussion.
- Final Notice (90+ days): Formal. State the account is being escalated to collections. Give a final 5-business-day window to resolve before collections action begins.

## Email Format
- Subject line should include company name and amount owed.
- Body should reference: customer name, outstanding amount, days overdue, and action requested.
- Sign off as "RPMX Construction — Accounts Receivable"
